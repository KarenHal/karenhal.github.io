GEOGRAPHY

 
Armenia was historiclly located between the Black, 
the Caspian and Mediterranian seas, comprising a 
territory of around 400.000 km2.Nowadays the 
Republic of Armenia is located in the North-East of 
ancient Armenia and shares a border with Georgia, 
Azerbaijan, Iran and Turkey.
 
Since Independence from the USSR in 1991 this small 
country of about 30.000 km2 has become an
industrious and modern nation capable of pleasing
the most demanding tourists.

CULTURE & TRADITIONS


The culture of Armenia encompasses many elements
that are based on the geography, literature, art, 
architecture, dance and music of the people. Armenia 
is an open air museum. Tourists can visit over 4,000
historical monuments throughout Armenia, from 
prehistoric to Hellenistic times, early to medieval
Christian era. With more than 50 fine arts museums 
and galleries. Armenian folk & classical music with
traditional instruments especially the duduk used in
many international films. Famous Armenian Artists
Aivazovsky, Saryan, Martirosyan & more. Folk dances
to theater. Armenia is a great destination for all.
