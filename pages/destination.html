Schedule

DAY 1.

Yerevan City tour. Echmiadzin. Zvartnots
Yerevan - one of the oldest cities in the world. Yerevan is the
capital of Armenia a metropolitan city with many colors,
great history, and unique culture.
With Jumpa tour you will see the spirit of our city.

DAY 2.

Yerevan. Arch of Charents. Garni. Geghard
One of the most popular routes. The Arch of Charents
named after the famous Armenian poet Eghishe Charents
who used to visit this site because of the breathtaking view
of Mount Ararat from this site. We will then visit the last
surviving pagan temple of Garni, which was built in the first
century AD. The village is also famous for its stones of
symphony, which is the result of nature’s creative work.
Continuing on our way we will reach the village of Goght,
founded in the 4th century monastery and rock-cut church
with spring of Geghard. Return to Yerevan.

DAY 3.

Yerevan. Sevan. Dilijan. Haghartsin. Goshavank
The first stop of the day will be Lake Sevan. The largest fresh
water lake in Armenia. The lake is completely surrounded by
impressive mountains and on the peninsula the 9th century
monastic complex of Sevanavank is the main attraction.
Then to Dilijan, many natural and historical attractions and
sanatoriums await us.

DAY 4.

Yerevan. Khor Virap. Areni. Noravank
Today we will discover the southern treasures of Armenia. A
visit to the 17th century Khor Virap monastery, with a
majestic view of Mount Ararat. We will continue through
many villages & reach the town of Areni famous for its fruits
and wines. Every year the wine festival is celebrated with
great splendor. The oldest shoe dating more than 3000
years has been discovered inside a cave in Areni. Then to
Noravank monastic complex. Masterpieces of Armenian
architecture a feast for the eyes with gorgeous surrounding
hills.
