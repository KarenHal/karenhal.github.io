Schedule

DAY 1.

Yerevan City Tour
Yerevan - one of the oldest cities in the world. Yerevan is the
capital of Armenia a metropolitan city with many colors and
unique architecture, cozy restaurants, cafés and beautiful
parks. With over 50 museums and good food. With Jumpa
tour you will see the spirit of our city.

DAY 2.

Gyumri City Tour. Marmashen

overnight in Gyumri

Gyumri is the second largest city in Armenia. It’s located in
the Shirak province. Northwest of the capital about an hour’s
drive. The city is known as the arts and crafts center. Because
of its music schools, theaters and performing arts. Tourist
can enjoy workshops with local artists; by creating pottery,
woodworking & painting. Gyumri is also home to several
monasteries, churches, museums and monuments.

DAY 3.

Lori Fortrass. Dendropark. Odzun

overnight in Dsegh

After having your breakfast we head to the 11th century Lori
Fortress. Located near the village of Lori Berd in the Lori
Province.
Dendropark of Stepanavan where more than 500 plant spe-
cies grows. Some were brought in from different countries &
some are wild local plants. An eco-tourist destination.
Odzun located in the Lori Province in the North of Armenia.
Overlooking the gorge & Debed River below, home of the
5th century basilica.

DAY 4.

Haghpat. Sanahin. Aghtala

back to Yerevan

The province of Lori in the North, is rich with magnificent
monasteries & evergreen trees.
Haghpat & Sanahin these two monasteries are UNESCO
heritage sites, with scriptorium belfry & churches. With
unique architecture developed from the 10th to the 13th
century.
Akhtala at 185 km North of Yerevan, the monastery of
Akhtala is very unique with its colorful murals. One of the
best representations of Byzantine art, similar to Armenian
miniature paintings of the 11th century manuscripts.
